About

Delivery/Collection Process

When you order a product, we calculate the distance between you and the farmer. If it is above 10KM, the item is marked for collection, otherwise the farmer will deliver it to your door. When an order is paid for, you will receive an email with the order details and an additional email for each farmer in the order who is more than 10KM away. The farmer will receive an email with your details and the delivery/collection status of the items.

Farmer Fees

When a customer buys your product you, will receive 95% of the purchase price. The rest is kept as a fee for the service and to cover transaction costs.

Farmer Payouts

Farmer orders are verified at the end of each month and our accounts team will contact you to arrange payouts. We are currently developing an automated payout system.
